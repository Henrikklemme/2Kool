We understand

As a founder duo, our families ran a bakery and a flower shop. We know the early mornings, the small margins, and how far down the list a website tends to sit.

So the site sits there, quietly ageing.

So we built this for businesses like our own families' — accessible, and priced so they could actually say yes.
